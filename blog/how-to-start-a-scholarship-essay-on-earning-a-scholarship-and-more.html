How To Start A Scholarship Essay On Earning A Scholarship And More

Acemyhomework Blog Mar 12, 2022

There are numerous scholarships being offered every year that go on to make it easier for students to pursue their education goals. However, so many other students will also want to get the scholarship, making the competition cutthroat. Besides, with thousands of applications submitted by students worldwide, reviewers may have a hard time reading through a boring introduction, and most likely, that essay ends up in the rejection pile. Therefore, part of writing a scholarship essay that elevates your chances of success is to have a killer introduction.

How To Start An Essay On Earning A Scholarship

Your introduction for scholarship essay should grab attention from the first sentence and make you stand out. Imagine the person awarding the scholarship has already read several applications that day and is tired. 

You don’t want your essay to give them a headache or for the person to start doubting whether or not you really deserve the money. So your essay for a scholarship should be short, easy to read, and understand while ensuring that you stand out from the rest of the applicants.

Start the essay with something exciting that will distinguish you from others. Create a personal connection and maintain your unique voice. Don’t hesitate to use literary devices such as a metaphor, anecdote, and simile. 

Alternatively, you can create a scenario that pulls the reader into your world and places. For this strategy to work, ensure it appeals to their senses and paints a vivid picture to make your story even more memorable. 

Avoid using quotes as they are other people’s words. In a scholarship, the reader is more interested in who you are, your passion, and your education goals. So use words that motivate the reviewer to continue reading until the last sentence.  To make your introduction great, ensure it has the following three essential components:

  • An attention-grabbing first sentence
  • A thesis statement that addresses the essay prompt
  • A brief explanation for what your essay is about

The start of your essay should help answer the question “why you deserve this scholarship while also maximizing the chances of getting the money. It should show your positive attitude, passion, uniqueness, and use language that creates an emotional impact. 

How Do You Introduce Yourself In A Scholarship?

One of the most popular scholarship essays prompts is “tell us about yourself.” To answer this prompt, you have to introduce yourself the right way without sounding arrogant. So consider your passion and education goals, then link them to the scholarship. 

Show the reviewer who you are and what drives you but make sure it relates to the goals and missions of the person or organization giving the scholarship. For example, when applying for a community service scholarship, briefly explain the kind of community service you do and why you are so passionate about it.

Other things that you might need to introduce yourself are:

  • Your short and long-term education/professional goals.
  • Your current education path and how it applies to your overall career goals.
  • Past experiences that pushed you to pursue a specific career path or those which sparked your passion.

Overall, if you choose to use a past experience, education goals, or professional aspirations, ensure they connect with the organization providing the financial aid. So even if a connection will paint you in a good way, don’t use it if it doesn’t fit naturally.

You can also mention hobbies and volunteer experiences that match what the review is looking for in an applicant. After the writing process is complete, take it a step further and review the text to ensure it is relevant to that organization and that the person reading it will surely find you a good fit for their scholarship. 

How Do You Write A Good Scholarship Essay?

Want to know to write a scholarship essay fast? Read on. The first advice that students are often given is to plan the essay far in advance. Waiting until the last few hours to write a scholarship essay will cause you to rush through the writing and editing process.

As a result, the quality will suffer. In such a scenario, you are better off handing the task to an experienced writer who will use the short time to come up with a flawless essay. But if you start early, here’s how to write an impressive scholarship essay.

Create a Good Structure

Once you start writing your scholarship essay early, you can create a good outline that ensures all the crucial points have been included. And you will even have enough time to polish the essay.

To create a great outline, list down all the points you want to include in your essay. Next, assign those ideas into their designated sections, be it in the introduction, body, or conclusion. Lastly, check to ensure the ideas have a logical order. Once you have an outline, the next step is to tailor your introduction to the audience. 

Use Real-Life Examples

When writing a winning scholarship, you have to show and not tell. That means you have to include real-life examples that paint a vivid picture of your experiences in a way that connects to the reader. Personal examples are a way to respond to the essay prompt in a unique manner and your chance to stand out. Avoid common or boring phrases because you want to grab attention from the word go. 

Proofread Thoroughly

After the writing process is complete, keep a distance, then read the essay aloud. Take your editing a step further and use available tools to polish your work. Finally, after proofreading it a few times, ask someone else to do it for you so that when you send the scholarship essay, you can be sure it is indeed perfect.
