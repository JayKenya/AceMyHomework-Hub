How Much Does Pearson Online Academy Cost? Tuition and Discounts

Acemyhomework Blog Mar 12, 2022

Learning is without a doubt a lifelong pursuit that can enable a person to acquire the knowledge and skills that are vital for individual and corporate success. Due to advancements in technology, learning is no longer confined within four walls of a classroom. An instructor with a textbook at hand standing behind a white or blackboard is no longer the sole source of educational experience. 

Online education has revolutionized how people learn and acquire skills because informative resources can be accessed from anywhere today. As it continues to gain popularity, more people are curious to know the cost of online education. Pearson Online Academy is a great example of an online school with affordable tuition rates. In this post, we'll take a look at the legitimacy, cost of tuition as well as discounts available for students at Pearson Online Academy.

Understanding E-learning

Technology has had a profound impact on the educational landscape. Online education is a modern way of acquiring knowledge and information via the internet. It allows students to learn at their own pace and in their environment. This makes it an ideal choice because of its flexibility in terms of time and place, especially among students who are unable to attend traditional classes due to work or family obligations. 

Considering an online school? You're not alone. In the age of the internet, more and more students are opting to get their education online. But with so many choices out there, it can be hard to know which one is right for you. Let’s take a closer look at one of the most popular digital schools in the marketplace known as the Pearson Online Academy.

What is Pearson Online Academy?

Pearson online academy is an online private school that provides a variety of courses to students all over the world. The academy provides online and blended learning solutions for students in grades K-12. These courses are offered in a variety of formats so that students can learn at their own pace and in their way. 

Since the program is online, learners have access to it from anywhere they have an internet connection. So, if you're looking for a flexible online learning environment that can be tailored to suit a learner’s individual needs, Pearson online academy may be just what you're looking for. Read on to find out more about what this academy has to offer.

Who Owns Pearson Online Academy?

Though it's one of the largest online education providers, many people are unaware of who owns Pearson Online Academy. Pearson Online Academy is a subsidiary of Pearson PLC, the world's largest education company. 

Is Pearson Online Accredited?

Many parents, guardians and learners struggle to find answers to the question “is Pearson Academy legit?” The answer to that question is dependent on the accreditation of the institution. Here's what you need to know about accreditation and why it matters. 

Accreditation is a process that assesses whether an educational institution meets certain standards. This process is important for several reasons: 

  • It assures students and families that the school meets basic standards of quality.
  • It provides employers with confidence that graduates have completed a recognized, high-quality program.
  • It can make schools eligible for certain types of financial aid.

And the answer is yes! Pearson Online Academy is accredited by AdvancED, the leading accreditation organization for online and blended learning programs. This means that the courses offered are recognized by colleges and universities worldwide. So, whether you're looking to continue your education after high school or simply want to get ahead, Pearson Online Academy can help you reach your goals. 

How Much Does Pearson Online Academy Cost?

If you are considering enrolling in Pearson Online Academy, you may be wondering how much the Pearson online academy tuition fees are. Fortunately, the price is very affordable, and there are a variety of payment plans available to make the program fit your budget. For instance, Pearson online academy offers monthly payment plans at 0% financing, and discounts for both full academic year and semester enrollment. 

These discounts can range from 5% to 10% off the tuition price. There is also a new family discount, which gives 5% off the tuition price. For active duty and retired military members, Pearson offers a 5%-10% discount on the tuition prices depending on the mode of study preferred. Additionally, the academy payment fee is charged vary on the program you choose and the amount of time you enrol in for. 

Have you ever wondered what the cost of Pearson online academy is for UK residents? Then you have probably searched for this “how much does Pearson online academy cost UK?” Well, there’s no need to wonder anymore because Pearson online academy is a digital learning platform that provides high-quality, effective, affordable, and accredited online education to students in the UK and beyond. 

In the UK, Pearson online academy is one of the most popular online academies, with courses that are carefully designed to help learners achieve their academic goals. Although the tuition fee is competitively priced, the school offers financial aid and discounts to their learner to enable them to get the education they need without breaking the bank. Students can choose from a variety of courses that suit their needs and interests, taught by experienced educators. 

Why Choose Pearson Online Academy?

Choosing an online academy can be a tough decision. Just as discussed, there are so many different factors to consider, like cost, curriculum, and accreditation. But when it comes to getting a quality education that will prepare you for success in college and beyond, Pearson Online Academy is a clear choice. Here's why:

  • The curriculum is standards-based and research-driven.
  • The school offers a variety of delivery methods, including synchronous and asynchronous learning.
  • The teachers are highly qualified and certified.
  • The learners have at their disposal resources such as tutoring, and counselling among others to help them succeed academically.
  • The school has a wide range of program options, including special education and credit recovery programs.
