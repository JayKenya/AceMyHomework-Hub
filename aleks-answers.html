Sample Organic Aleks Chemistry Questions

Question 1: Which functional group is present in alcohols?

A) Carboxyl group B) Hydroxyl group C) Amino group D) Carbonyl group

Answer: B) Hydroxyl group is present in alcohols. It is a functional group consisting of an oxygen atom bonded to a hydrogen atom and is characteristic of alcohols.

Question 2: What is the general formula for alkanes?

A) CnH2n B) CnH2n+2 C) CnHn D) CnH2n-2

Answer: B) The general formula for alkanes is CnH2n+2. Alkanes are a family of hydrocarbons with single covalent bonds and follow this formula.

Question 3: Which of the following compounds is an example of an aromatic hydrocarbon?

A) Ethane (C2H6) B) Benzene (C6H6) C) Propane (C3H8) D) Butene (C4H8)

Answer: B) Benzene (C6H6) is an example of an aromatic hydrocarbon. It is a cyclic compound with alternating double bonds and is highly stable and unique in its chemical properties.

Question 4: What is the primary functional group in carboxylic acids?

A) Carbonyl group B) Hydroxyl group C) Carboxyl group D) Amine group

Answer: C) Carboxyl group is the primary functional group in carboxylic acids. It consists of a carbonyl group (C=O) and a hydroxyl group (OH) attached to the same carbon atom.

Question 5: Which of the following is a secondary alcohol?

A) Ethanol (CH3CH2OH) B) Propanol (CH3CH2CH2OH) C) Methanol (CH3OH) D) Isopropanol (CH3CHOHCH3)

Answer: D) Isopropanol (CH3CHOHCH3) is a secondary alcohol. It has two alkyl groups attached to the carbon atom bearing the hydroxyl group.
